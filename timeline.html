Americans forced to surrender gold at $20.67/ounce. Price revalued to $35/ounce days later - a 40% devaluation of savings. Executive Order 6102 criminalized private gold ownership, transferring wealth from citizens to government.

1945-1959

1,600+ Nazi scientists brought to America, given new identities. Foundation of NASA, CIA mind-control programs, and military-industrial complex. So-called war criminals like Wernher von Braun utilized for their expertise, contingent on their silence about the truth of the war, and the exaggeration of the Holocaust.

CIA/MI6 overthrow Iran's democratically elected Prime Minister Mohammad Mosaddegh after he nationalized oil. Operation involved propaganda, bribery, and staged protests. Established the Shah's brutal regime, leading directly to 1979 Iranian Revolution.

CIA documents released 2013
1953-1973

Project MKUltra

CIA's mind control program testing LSD and other methods on unwitting citizens. At least 80 institutions participated, including universities and hospitals. Most records destroyed in 1973 by order of Director Richard Helms.

Partial documents released 1977 via FOIA
1956-1971

COINTELPRO

FBI program to surveil, infiltrate, discredit, and disrupt domestic political organizations. Targeted civil rights leaders (MLK, Malcolm X), anti-war activists, feminist groups, and communist organizations. Methods included blackmail, false accusations, and psychological warfare.

Exposed by Citizens' Commission 1971
1962

U.S. Joint Chiefs propose false-flag terrorist attacks on American cities to blame Cuba. Plans included hijacking planes, sinking ships, and orchestrating violent incidents. Signed by all Joint Chiefs but rejected by President Kennedy.

Declassified 1997 via ARRB
1963

Records partially released via ARRB

Dubious reports of North Vietnamese attacks lead to Congressional resolution escalating Vietnam War. Declassified documents reveal false radar contacts and questionable interpretations. 58,000 Americans and millions of Vietnamese die in subsequent war.

Israeli forces attack U.S. Navy intelligence ship for 75 minutes during Six-Day War. 34 American sailors killed, 171 wounded. Johnson administration covers up attack, orders survivors to remain silent. Declassified NSA intercepts confirm deliberate attack.

"Nixon Shock" removes last brake on money printing. U.S. dollar becomes pure fiat currency, enabling unlimited debt creation and inflation. Petrodollar system established, forcing oil trade in dollars to maintain demand for currency.
